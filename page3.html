Data Visualisation

In response to our project's topic：There is a lack of female role models in sports, we designed questionnaires and collected data to gain information, which can help prove this opinion.

Since there are too many sports, in order to facilitate our research, we first tried to make a reasonable restriction on sports items. We chose the first three sports which have highly global influence, football, basketball and tennis (Benjamin,2018).

In order to find out whether there is a gap in public recognition between male and female athletes, we connected to the brief and selected the following 6 athletes from the endorser list of adidas to conduct research.
It is obvious from the chart that the public is more familiar with male athletes. This seems to support the opinion that female athletes and female athlete role models tend to be invisible.
However, it is worth noticed that, in tennis where female players can receive more adequate coverage in the media(Yip, 2018), female athlete gained as much public recognition as male athlete.This seems to indicate the relationship between media coverage and the visibility of athletes. The media coverage has a great influence on the public recognition of athletes.

These data still underscore that the media coverage has a great influence on the public recognition of athletes. At the same time, it also reflects the role of media in shaping athletes' role models.

Number of competitions watched last year

According to the data collected from our questionnaire, it can be seen that there is still a gap in the public's attention to male and female competitions. It's also a sign that female sport events, female athletes, and female role models are not getting enough attention.

Attention degree of the characteristics of role models

There are some researchers claimed that female athletes and women’s sport are still sadly under-represented in all types of media, and sportswomen are rarely represented solely for their athletic abilities. Instead, the focus is often on their physical appearance and femininity. (Fink, 2013).
And from our data, it can be seen that the public tends to pay more attention on the appearance of female athletes.
This data may support the above opinion and also emphasize the role of the media in shaping the image of female athletes.
